about the project

concept

The project describes a new, emerging way to shed light on a Stakeholders dataset and to communicate the findings with a broad audience. I begin this process with understanding of the data followed by KPI creation, data modelling, data cleaning and embed them within a narrative using infographics. I have created this end-to-end project in Microsoft Excel. I illustrate the concepts of a business to help sales team to summarize what happened and know where the strong and weak products and sales areas. In this paper, i have described in detail the process by which i explored the data and built the visualizations that underlie that work.

data

The dataset is related to the stakeholders, which comes under the Sales domain. It is a huge dataset which is divided into different tables where each table contains its releveant information. The dataset includes the information related to the Business Transactions, stores, region, stock keeping units, salesman and period.

Inference

Below are the inferences which i analyzed from the dataset

  • Gecko Store is having maximum sales of $860 while Storeclean is having minimum sales of $-190
  • The Northern region is having maximum sales of $8,388 and the Eastern region is having the minimum sales of $973. No region can achieve the sales target
  • Shweta Kalla salesman is having maximum sales of $1,900 and the minimum sales is of Wahid Khan $1,222
  • Pre-Covid sales are more than the post covid-sales
  • In year 2020 April month is having the maximum sales of $1,659 and in year 2018 October month is having minimum sales of $8. Targeted sales cannot be achieved in noone of the year
  • The customers visit is maximum during the pre-covid period of 71.27%. The customer visits target has been achieved during the pre-covid period, but in the post-covid period it cannot be achieved
  • The Spring season is having the maximum sales of $4,987, while Summer is having minimum sales of $3,369
  • The Garnier products is having maximum sales of 48%, while NYX professional is having minimum sales of 18%

process

In order to analyze the sales data and generate insights out of it, i followed the a process that looked at the data using the following visualizations:

  • Storewise sales
  • Regionwise Sales
  • Top 3 Salesman
  • Pre-Post Covid-19 Sales
  • Year & Monthwise Sales
  • Pre-Post Covid-19 Visits
  • Seasonwise Sales
  • Productwise Sales
