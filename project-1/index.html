about the project

The Consumer Behavior and Shopping Habits Dataset provides comprehensive insights into consumers' preferences, tendencies, and patterns during their shopping experiences. It includes demographic information, purchase history, product preferences, and preferred shopping channels (online or offline). This dataset is essential for businesses aiming to tailor their strategies to meet customer needs and enhance their shopping experience, ultimately driving sales and loyalty.
I begin the process with understanding of the data and perform exploratory data analysis in SQL and created a dashboard in Power BI tool. The data is taken from the Kaggle website and it contains 3900k records and 19 columns.

process

In order to analyze the data and generate insights out of it, i followed the a process that looked at the data using the following visualizations:

  • Season wise sales
  • Purchase by Payment Methods
  • Gender wise Purchasing
  • Color wise items purchased
  • Top 5 items
  • Age group wise items purchased
  • Shipping type by location
  • Gender wise payment

Inference

Below are the inferences which i analyzed from the dataset

  • Fall season is having the highest sales of 60k and lowest sales of 56k.
  • Credit card purchasing method is highest and Debit card is lowest
  • United States is having maximum Tv shows and movies.
  • Males have done maximum purchases.
  • Olive color items are most purchased and gold is lowest.
  • Top 5 items purchased are jewellery, pants, blouse, dress and sweaters.
  • Age group of people having 36-45 is having maximum items purchased while 70-above age group have purchased minimum items.s
  • Free Shipping service is given in maximum locations and 2-day service is given in minimum locations.
  • Male gender has done the maximum payment.
