About The Dataset

It is a Finance dataset for the Bank Loan which includes the information about the loan amount, interest rate, installment, loan status etc. It includes two different datasets, where each dataset holds 39k+ records. The dataset The client wants to analyse the loan for its customers. I begin the process with understanding of the data followed by KPI creation, data modelling, data cleaning and embed them within a narrative using infographics. I have created this end-to-end project in Power BI. In this paper, i have described in detail the process by which i explored the data and built the visualizations that underlie that work.

Inferences

Below are the inferences analyzed from the data

  • Bank may generate profit more profit year on year.
  • "CA state" is having highest number of accounts in each type of loan status and "ME state" is having lowest number of accounts.
  • The total payment for verified account is higher compared to non-verified. 58.88% are the verified accounts, remaining 41.12% are non-verified.
  • The Home ownership varies from 2008 to 2016 and the maximum amount reaches in year 2012, its around 31M and the minimum amount is in 2008.
  • The highest number of fully paid accounts is in the month of March and the lowest is in the month of September.
  • The average revolving balance is higher in the 'grade B' its around 29M and the maximum reaches in the 'subgrade B1' that is 39M, Same like the average lower revolving balance is in 'grade G' its around 1200k and the minimum reaches in the 'subgrade G5' that is 701K.
